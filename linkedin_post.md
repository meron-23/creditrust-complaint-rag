# High-Impact LinkedIn Post: CrediTrust Financial Complaint Intelligence AI

**Headline: Transforming Raw Customer Feedback into Strategic FinTech Insights with RAG and Google Gemini**

Customer complaints are often seen as a burden, but for high-growth FinTechs like CrediTrust Financial, they are a strategic goldmine.

I’m excited to share my latest project: an **AI-Powered Complaint Intelligence System** specifically designed for the East African financial market. 

By leveraging **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** and the power of **Google Gemini**, I’ve built a tool that allows Product Managers and Compliance teams to query thousands of unstructured customer narratives in plain English and receive synthesized, evidence-backed business reports in seconds.

**Key Technical Highlights:**
- **Semantic Search**: Powered by FAISS and Sentence-Transformers for lightning-fast retrieval of relevant customer pain points.
- **Actionable Intelligence**: Integrated Google Gemini 1.5 Flash to synthesize raw narratives into formal business recommendations.
- **Enterprise-Grade UI**: A minimalist, professional interface built with Streamlit—optimized for C-Suite decision-making (and zero emojis!).
- **Regional Context**: Dynamic metadata tracking across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda to identify market-specific trends.

**The Business Value:**
Shift from reactive firefighting to proactive product innovation. This tool empowers stakeholders like Asha (BNPL Product Manager) to spot emerging payment processing issues in Nairobi or Dar es Salaam before they impact the bottom line.
